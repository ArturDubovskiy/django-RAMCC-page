Founder and leader

This organization is a non-profit, non-political, non-religious. The organization brings together in its ranks on a voluntary basis lovers of motorcycles (motorcycles, scooters, mopeds and ATV). The Falcons Moto Club is open to a wide range of people (including people with disabilities) who have a legal motorcycle, the appropriate driver's category, or who want to become the owner of the right motorcycle category in the future and finally realize themselves in this movement.

Targets and activity:
  • The unification of people close in spirit and interests to motorcycle trips and love to motorcycles, wishing to realize themselves in this movement;
  • Support for people with disabilities who are interested in motorcycles and want to participate in the development of motorcycle movements;
  • Maintenance, repair, tuning and construction of motorcycles, exchange of experience; Propaganda of the motor movement culture and the formation of a positive public opinion about its representatives;
  • Promotion of Ukrainian cultural heritage;
  • Organization of motor races, events, rallies;
  • Making a contribution to the national Ukrainian culture of motorsport;
  • Cooperation with other motor clubs;
  • Support for motorcyclists on trips, runs and other activities;
  • Cooperation with the legislature of the Ukraine or any other country in which the club members are staying;
  • Cultural, social, scientific, educational, charitable and other purposes;
  • Formation of the general culture of the individual, the adaptation of the individual to life in society;
  • Creating the basis for the informed choice and development of professional motorcycle management skills;
  • Promotion of competent and trouble-free driving. The study and compliance with the rules of the road. Mutual aid on the road;
  • The implementation of other activities not prohibited by applicable law and aimed at achieving statutory goals and objectives ..
